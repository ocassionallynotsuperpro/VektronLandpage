About Us

General Petroleum is a leading provider of petroleum products and industrial solutions. Established in 1990, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation in the petroleum industry.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality products and services that meet the specific needs of our clients. We work with businesses across various sectors, including manufacturing, transportation, construction, and energy.
